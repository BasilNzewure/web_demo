Web Development

Website development is the process of creating, building, and maintaining websites and web applications through coding, structuring, and content management. It combines front-end (user-facing layout/interactivity) and back-end (server-side logic/databases) development to create functional digital experiences, often utilizing languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and PHP.

Software Development

Software development is the systematic process of conceiving, designing, programming, testing, and maintaining software applications, frameworks, or components. It involves a lifecycle (SDLC) that transforms user needs into functional, secure digital products, ranging from mobile apps to operating systems. Key methodologies include Agile and DevOps.

Data Science

Data science is the interdisciplinary field that involves the extraction of knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data. It combines statistics, mathematics, and computer science to analyze large datasets, identify patterns, and make data-driven decisions. Key techniques include machine learning, data visualization, and statistical modeling.
